WHY DANCE?

I like to dance because it makes them feel good about myself mentally and physically, is a form of self-expression, and is a way to express my innermost thoughts and emotions. Dance is also entertainment for me because i find joy in just watching various forms of dance.

It's a lifelong relationship where the love of your life is :

  • Aways there when you need.
  • Always always manages to make you smile when you are having a low day.
  • Never lets you down.
  • Makes you the happiest you have ever been.
  • Has been with you through all your most precious memories because let's face it, they're mostly dance related.
